Talks: (3:00 - 5:00 PM)
---

1. Introduction to IPython - Beginner level - [Kracekumar](https://twitter.com/kracetheking) - 30 minutes.

2.  Intro Pycassa - [Shoaib](https://www.meetup.com/BangPypers/members/78184512/)
